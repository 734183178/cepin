4 Scientific Dimensions: Comprehensive Relationship Psychology Assessment

Each dimension is carefully designed to analyze your psychological state in romantic relationships from different angles

01

Control Desire Index

Evaluates whether you try to control your partner's behavior, decisions, and social circle. Excessive control can make your partner feel suffocated; you need to learn to respect their independent space

02

Jealousy Intensity Level

Measures your sensitivity to your partner's interactions with the opposite sex. Moderate jealousy shows care, but excessive jealousy can destroy trust and cause unnecessary conflicts

03

Emotional Dependence Level

Detects your level of mental dependence on your partner. Excessive dependence can lead to losing yourself and creating imbalanced relationships; maintaining appropriate independence makes love more lasting

04

Relationship Security

Measures your level of anxiety and unease in the relationship. Lack of security is often the root cause of possessiveness; finding inner stability is the key breakthrough
